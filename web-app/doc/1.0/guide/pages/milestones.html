1.2 Milestones

From September 2012 - pilot phase

Mets production

Will have a service to generate XML Mets documents ( profile 3 and 5; logical map ). Logical relations are often expressed in the flat CSV documents that a digital scanning factory delivers to a content provider ( when it mass produces high quality imagery ). This will be the source for Mets document production.

The Mets document that is fabricated this way can be downloaded via the dissemination API.

Mets can already be ingested by a content provider.

Rendering of the logical map

Ingested Mets will be used:
  • by the derivative service to produce a PDF
  • to extend it's given access policy ( open, restricted and closed ) to all master and derivative material.
  • Display the logical relationships of the files at the dissemination API

Video and audio derivative

Will have a service extension to produce lower level copies and preview renderings of audio and video content.

Version 3 alpha

As from December 2013 Ready for production

Version 4

Will have integrity validation of stored master material. This involves random checksum calculations on files. When file degradation is detected reparations are performed.
